My Autobiography

My name is Nurshazlyatul Binti Mohamad Nasir, and I was born and raised in Kangar Perlis. I am 21 years old and was born on 26th July 2003. From an early age, I've always been fascinated by the world around me, constantly seeking to understand how things work and why people behave the way they do. This curiosity has driven me to explore various fields of interest and led me down numerous exciting paths in life.

Growing up in a small town, I was surrounded by close-knit communities and nature. My childhood was filled with adventures in the woods, playing sports with friends, and spending quality time with my family. My parents, who are both educators, instilled in me the importance of learning and curiosity. They encouraged me to pursue my interests, no matter how unconventional they seemed at the time.

As I continue on my journey, I am committed to continuous learning and personal growth. I believe that life is about exploring new horizons, both within and outside of ourselves. I am constantly seeking to understand more about the world and my place in it, striving to make a positive impact in everything I do.

Looking ahead as I continue on my journey, I am excited about the future and the endless possibilities it holds. I am committed to continuous learning, personal growth, and making a positive impact on the world around me. This journal is a space where I will share my memories, experiences, and interests, and I hope it resonates with those who read it.

My Goals 💫

As a student, I am constantly thinking about my future and the goals and dreams that drive me. These aspirations shape my daily activities, influence my choices, and give me a sense of purpose. My goals and dreams encompass both my academic and personal life, and they are the guiding forces that keep me motivated and focused.

Academic Goals📚

One of my primary goals as a student is to excel academically. I strive to maintain a high GPA and develop a deep understanding of the subjects I am passionate about. Currently, I am majoring in Library Management at Universiti Teknologi MARA Kedah branch in Sungai Petani campus. My goal is to not only grasp the theoretical aspects of my field but also to apply this knowledge in practical settings.

To achieve this, I am committed to actively participating in my classes, engaging in discussions, and seeking help whenever necessary. I believe in the importance of continuous learning and often go beyond the classroom material to explore additional resources such as online courses, books, and research papers. One of my dreams is to contribute to my field by conducting meaningful research and sharing my findings with the academic community.

Personal Goals🧕

In addition to my academic and career aspirations, I have several personal goals that are important to me. One of my key personal goals is to develop a well-rounded skill set that includes not only technical expertise but also soft skills such as communication, leadership, and teamwork. I am actively involved in various extracurricular activities, including The Malaysian Red Crescent (MRC), which help me build these skills and contribute to my personal growth.

In conclusion, my goals and dreams as a student are multifaceted and encompass various aspects of my life. I am driven by a desire to excel academically, build a successful career, develop a well-rounded skill set, and make a positive impact on society. These aspirations guide my actions and decisions, and I am dedicated to working hard to achieve them.

My Childhood Memories👶 👧

Childhood is a magical time, filled with endless adventures, innocent joys, and moments that leave a lasting impression. As I look back on my early years, I am flooded with memories that bring a smile to my face and warmth to my heart. These memories are not just recollections of the past. They are the building blocks of who I am today.

Early Years👶 👧

Growing up in Kampung Sungai Bakong Kangar, Perlis, my childhood was surrounded by the beauty of nature with full of wonderful views of paddy fields and hills also the warmth of a close-knit community. My days were filled with laughter, curiosity, and exploration. One of my earliest memories is playing in the backyard with my siblings and friends. We would create imaginary worlds, build forts out of blankets and sticks, and embark on grand adventures without ever leaving our yard.

Neighborhood Adventures 👧

The neighborhood where I grew up was a playground in itself. We lived in village, and all the kids from nearby houses would gather to play games like tag, hide and seek, and kickball. I remember riding my bike with my neighbor, Alia Syahirah, to the local park, where we would spend hours on the swings and slides, lost in the simplicity of childhood fun.
